Chandra Shekhar Rajan

C S Rajan is an an IAS officer of the 1978 batch who retired as Chief Secretary, Rajasthan in 2016. Since then he has been serving as Deputy Chairman, Chief Minister’s Advisory Council. During his years in the IAS he has served in various capacities in agriculture and rural development, before spending 12 years in infrastructure sectors like power, roads, industries and 5 years in finance and general administration, respectively. He has co-authored a book on ‘Farmers Participation in Agricultural Research and Extension’. He has also briefly served as a Consultant with the World Bank. In October 2018, he was appointed by the Union Government as Director on the Board of IL&FS and since April 2019, he has been serving as MD, IL&FS.
